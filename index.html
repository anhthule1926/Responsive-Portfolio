About Me

I was born in Vietnam. My family is consist of my mom, brothers and sister. My older brother and my dad left Vietnam when I was 2, a year later my mom decided that she to take my sister, brother and I and left Vietnam. I was 3 years old when we left Vietnam. We along with other people left Vietnam in a small boat in the middle of the night.

We were stranded for days in the ocean before we made it Malaysia. Once we maded it to Malaysia we were placed in the refugee camp and spent the next 5 years there. We were luck enough to come to the US in 1994, beause after we left the all of the refugee camps got closed down.

Becuase of where I came from and background I love to travel and experience new things. I have done zip lining, parasailing, swimming with the turtles, 13 miles hike to Havasupai, and have travel to so many places. I love being expose to new culture and food.
