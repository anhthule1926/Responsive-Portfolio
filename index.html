About Me

I was born in Vietnam. My family is consist of my mom, brothers and sister. My older brother and my dad left Vietnam when I was 2, a year later my mom decided that she to take my sister, brother and I and left Vietnam. I was 3 years old when we left Vietnam. We along with other people left Vietnam in a small boat in the middle of the night.

We were stranded for days in the ocean before we made it Malaysia. Once we maded it to Malaysia we were placed in the refugee camp and spent the next 5 years there. My mom had to go through an interview process before we could come to the US and re-united with my brother.

Since coming to the US, I have spend most of my life growing up in Minnesota. No matter where we were planning to move to we always came back to Minnesota, because it's home.
